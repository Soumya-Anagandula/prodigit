Linkin Science has successfully completed 3rd Edition of Advancements of Lasers, Optics and Photonics as a Virtual Conference during September 01-02, 2021. We thank all our expertise Scientific Committee Members and speakers who offered a constant support for the conference.

The Global Excellence Awards for 3rd Edition of Advancements of Laser, Optics and photonics 2021 are:

  • Aurel Ymeti, NanoBio Research Group, Saxion University of Applied Sciences & Nanoalmyona BV, Netherlands (Best Research)
  • Shobha Shukla, Indian Institute of Technology (IIT- Mumbai) (Young Researcher Award)
  • Vanessa De Souza, UNINOVE Nove de Julho University, Brazil (Best Poster)
